## Summary

Entire launched in February 2026 with a $60 million seed round and their first product, Checkpoints. The announcement post is a hello-world introduction — the company describes itself as building the future of software development, with Checkpoints as the initial shipping product.

A $60M seed is a notable signal: it's among the larger seed rounds in the current wave of AI coding agent infrastructure companies. The investor conviction suggests Entire is positioning for a significant platform play, not just a tooling niche. The \Checkpoints\ product name is suggestive of state management or save-point functionality in an agent-assisted development workflow, though the announcement post doesn't go deep on product details.

This sits alongside other well-capitalized developer tools companies in the agent space — Cursor (significant funding), Anysphere, the various cloud agent orchestration platforms. The concentration of capital in this sector reflects how early the infrastructure still is.

## Key points

- Entire launched with $60M seed round in February 2026.
- First product: Checkpoints — AI-assisted software development tooling.
- One of the larger seed rounds in the AI coding agent infrastructure space.
- Product details sparse at launch; the announcement is primarily a public introduction.
