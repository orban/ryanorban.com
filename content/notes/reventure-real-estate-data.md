## Summary

Reventure is a real estate data analytics platform with the tagline "Real Estate Insights in Minutes." It provides market-level analysis — price trends, affordability metrics, inventory levels, rental yields — visualized across US markets for investors and homebuyers trying to make data-driven decisions.

The platform is associated with Nick Gerli and the Reventure Consulting brand, which has built a following by providing skeptical, data-anchored analysis of US housing markets at a time when housing affordability has become a major economic and political issue. The approach contrasts with the typical real estate broker perspective (bullish by default) by using actual price-to-income ratios, days-on-market trends, and other metrics that give a clearer picture of market health.

For individual investors evaluating markets for rental property or house hacking, the kind of market-level data Reventure provides is more actionable than local anecdote. Understanding whether a city's price-to-rent ratio makes buying or renting more rational, or whether inventory is rising or falling, are the decisions that actually matter for investment outcomes.

## Key points

- Real estate market analytics across US cities — price trends, affordability, inventory, rental yields.
- Associated with Nick Gerli and data-skeptical housing market commentary.
- Provides price-to-income ratios, days-on-market, and other fundamentals-based metrics.
- Contrasts with conventional broker optimism: data-first perspective on market health.
- Target audience: individual investors and serious homebuyers making market-selection decisions.
