## Summary

This is a myNoise vlog episode by Dr. Ir. Stéphane Pigeon, the creator of myNoise — the web's foremost ambient sound generator. The episode explores the world of harmonics through a personal lens: coffee, guitar, and synthesizer as vehicles for understanding how complex sounds are built from fundamental frequencies and their overtones.

Harmonics are the integer multiples of a fundamental frequency that give instruments and voices their timbre. A guitar and a synthesizer playing the same note sound different because they have different harmonic profiles — different relative strengths of the same overtones. Understanding this is foundational to sound design, mixing, and acoustic science.

Stéphane Pigeon brings an unusual combination of credentials — audio engineering and signal processing — to what is also an accessible personal format. The vlog episodes on myNoise sit at the intersection of technical depth and auditory fascination that characterizes the platform itself.
