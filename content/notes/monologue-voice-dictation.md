## Summary

Monologue is an AI-powered voice dictation tool for macOS and iOS that adapts to your writing style, vocabulary, and the application you're writing in. The context-awareness is the main differentiator: it doesn't just transcribe — it formats text appropriately for Slack vs email vs a code editor, and learns your domain-specific vocabulary and mixed-language expressions through an auto dictionary.

Privacy is a core design constraint: no audio files stored on servers, zero LLM data retention, custom vocabulary modes stay on-device. This positions it against cloud dictation services that retain training data. 100+ language support. Works via a keyboard shortcut in any application.

Monologue is part of the Every bundle ($30/month), which includes several AI productivity apps from the Every team. Standalone Pro is $144/year. The Every bundle positioning means it's not a standalone product so much as a component in a curated AI tools subscription. Compare with Spokenly (more standalone, local models) and Handy (minimal, open-source).

## Key points

- Context-aware formatting: adapts output to the app you're writing in (Slack, email, code, etc.).
- Auto dictionary: learns your unique vocabulary and mixed-language expressions.
- Privacy-first: no audio retained, no LLM training on your data, custom modes on-device.
- 100+ languages; keyboard-shortcut invocation from any application.
- Every bundle ($30/month) or standalone Pro ($144/year).
- Compare: Spokenly (local models, agent mode), Handy (minimal, fully open-source).
