## Summary

Awakening Bell is a web-based tool that rings a bell sound at configurable intervals, serving as a reminder to pause and breathe consciously. The practice comes from Thich Nhat Hanh's mindfulness tradition: when a bell sounds, you stop what you're doing, breathe in and out three times, and return to the present moment before continuing.

The tool offers periodic bells at set minute intervals, random intervals within a range, hourly bells, and custom reminders. Two bell sounds are available — small bell and large bell. No account, no app install, runs in the browser.

The simplicity is the point. Unlike meditation apps with guided sessions, streaks, and gamification, this is a single-function tool: make a sound at intervals. It's closer to a physical bell in a meditation hall than a productivity app.

## Key points

- Periodic, random, hourly, and custom bell configurations.
- Two bell sounds: small bell and large bell.
- Browser-based, no account required.
- Inspired by Thich Nhat Hanh's bell tradition in Plum Village Buddhism.
- No gamification, no tracking — single-function mindfulness reminder.
