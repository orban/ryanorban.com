## Summary

hackernews.betacat.io uses ChatGPT to generate summaries of top Hacker News stories and discussions, producing a daily digest that captures the key points from both the linked article and the HN comment thread. The output lets you stay current with what the tech community is discussing without reading through hundreds of comments.

The value is time compression. Hacker News comment threads at their best are high-signal: practitioners debating the technical merits of a paper, founders sharing context on a startup announcement, engineers surfacing counterexamples to a trending take. At their worst they're noise. An LLM-generated summary can extract the consensus and key disagreements without requiring an hour in the comments.

Similar tools include tldr.tech (newsletter format), hntldr.com (thread-specific summaries), and Briefing (morning news digest). The betacat version is notable for applying ChatGPT to both the article and the comment discussion — the combination gives more context than article-only summarization.

## Key points

- Daily AI-powered digest of top Hacker News stories and comment discussions.
- Uses ChatGPT to summarize both linked articles and HN comment threads.
- Compresses the signal from HN without requiring time in the comments.
- Part of a broader category of AI-powered news aggregation tools.
- Related: tldr.tech, hntldr.com (per-thread summaries).
