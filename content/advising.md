I love helping builders — but meaningful help needs context. I take on a small number of formal advising relationships each quarter.

## Who this is for

- Early teams with a real wedge (even if messy)
- Repeat founders pressure-testing a new story
- Technical leaders navigating AI/data/product tradeoffs

## Ways to work together

### Quick Consult (60 min)

One deep dive + written notes within 24h. $500–$750 flat (or equivalent in credits).

### Advisor (Equity)

1–2h/mo, async access, selective intros, quarterly focus blocks. 0.25–1.0% common, 12-mo vest, 3-mo cliff.

### Advisor (Cash)

Same scope when equity isn't feasible. $2–3k/mo.

### Founder Coaching

Leadership, narrative, decision-loops. 60 min every other week; $250/session.

## What I do

- Sharpen positioning and fundraising story
- Design PMF experiments and early metrics
- Review product/infra choices (esp. AI/RAG/security)
- Serve as thinking partner for gnarly decisions

## Expectations

- You share context in advance; I show up prepared and direct
- Communication is async-first (email/Slack/Notion)
- Either side can pause with one week's notice
