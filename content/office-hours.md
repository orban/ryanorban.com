I get a lot of "quick chat" requests. To keep things useful, I batch them into short office hours once a week.

Use this for one focused question on: early PMF signals, GTM wedge, fundraising narrative, AI/data infra choices, or career navigation.

## How it works

- 20 minutes, video or phone
- Bring one question and 1–2 links max
- Not for: blind intros, full deck reviews, or NDA topics
- If we need more depth, I'll point you to the advisory path

[Book a slot](https://calendly.com/ryan-orban-9e09/30min)

Can't find a time? Check back next week; slots refresh.

## What to prep

- 1-sentence problem → 1-sentence desired outcome
- Current constraint (market, product, team, capital)
- "What would make this 20 minutes a win?"
